Transformations

May 20th, 2013

Even though I do not foresee myself using much transformations for future front-end development, I think there will some opportunities where I could surely implement transformations. An example I can think about is creating email template or even Christmas card to Grandma. I think there are certainly cases where these transformations will be useful but I will not know until I experiment. Head on over to check out my work and development.

I broke my transformation experimentation into two sections, two dimensional and three dimensional. I personally think its even more confusing when I try to use the shorthand method for transform. I think with more practice and time, I will get use to it. Even though I have just posted up some examples, I will continually expand on these sections over time. With my Starter League class moving so quickly, along with schoolwork, it is quite hard to sit down and fully enjoy everything. I do like the fast pace though since it gives me the chance to see more and return to them more. By practicing these skills trough these creations and experimentations, I think my experience learning HTML/CSS has opened up doors and has allowed me to explore more routes. A great example was when I learned jQuery and a little of JavaScript. Now I am just going bananas over those two.
