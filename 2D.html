Inkprinted

India Ink mixed with torn paper compiled onto one and later attached to the wall. Time dried the remaining water away from the paper and the print fell off. Hence, leaving an imprint and a mark. December 2012.

Faces of Politics

This work came about with my interest in the 2012 election presidential election. Time Magazine was my main source of collage. I would cut out various politicians and compile a set before downsizing it 200 images. I later scanned the images and ran a program. The left image has no change, no blending, and no adjustments. On the right is a product that blends 100% of the politicians I have scanned in. This work was originally printed on standard letter size paper. Ideally, I would like to print out 100,000 to track the subtle variations but that would kill a lot trees.
